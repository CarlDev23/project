Order

An order on our website refers to a formal request made by a customer to purchase a specific product or service listed on our platform. By adding selected items to the virtual shopping cart and completing the checkout process, customers initiate an order. The order includes product details, quantities, pricing, and any applicable discounts or promotional offers. including the estimated delivery date and any relevant tracking information. Our team will process the order swiftly and ensure timely delivery to the designated shipping address.

Delivered

"Delivered" status on our website indicates that the ordered product has successfully reached the specified delivery address and is now in the possession of the customer. Once the delivery is marked as "delivered," customers should inspect the package immediately to ensure the contents are in good condition and to report any issues promptly.We take utmost care in ensuring that the products are securely delivered to the designated address within the estimated timeframe.

Shipping

Once an order is confirmed and payment is processed, our dedicated logistics team carefully packs and labels the products for shipment. We work with trusted shipping partners to ensure the safe and timely delivery of your orders. Upon shipment, customers receive a shipping confirmationPlease note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and any unforeseen circumstances, such as weather conditions or logistical challenges.
